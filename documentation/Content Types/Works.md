Works are performance pieces. That is a play, concerto, ballet, etc. Works have meta data such as a title, creator(s) _(playwright, composer, choreographer, etc.)_, the type of work it is _(e.g. dance, musical composition, etc.)_, description, and other relevant information. [_Swan Lake_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swan_Lake), [_Die Fledermaus_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Die_Fledermaus), and [_The Misanthrope_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Misanthrope) are all examples works.
